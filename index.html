About Me

Hello! I am Charlotte Zhou. I am a Psychology graduate from San Francisco State University, and am trying to break into the technology industry. I am currently a part of the Registered Pre-Apprenticeship Program with Dev/Mission. The program is filled with classes learning about IoT, IT essentials, and programming. I have always had an interest in technology, and am glad to finally be pursuing that interest. For the past couple years, I have been a caregiver for my grandma, which has been a great experience, but I want to build a career for myself, so I have high hopes for what will come out of completing this program.

I do still enjoy the field of Psychology, but because I want to enter Technology, I want to find a happy medium between the two, which is where my interest in the field of IT (Information Technology) comes from, because it would be exactly that. I will continue to hone my skills in IT, and keep working towards a future in that field.
